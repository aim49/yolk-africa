Why Yolk?

Yolk is the coloured internal part of an egg; jam packed with important nutrients that nourishes the developing embryo. And if prepared for a meal, yolk contain vitamins that provides energy required by human body and other animals for daily optimal functioning.

With positive changes that comes by consuming the egg-yolk, to us yolk becomes a perfect symbol for a change, a catalyst that functions to enrich and nourish socially and economically deprived people and communities.

Our Vision

Socio-economic enriched societies

Our Mission

To breakout hidden potential of people and communities at the backyards of societies; subjected to extreme forms of social and economic deprivation, by offering a springboard that enrich, nourish and catalyze a change.

Our Story

Yolk Africa was founded by Gram Makwarimba in 2014 to enrich people and communities at the margins of society to overcome social and economic discrimination with a primary focus on widows and single mothers. The purpose of Yolk Africa is achieved through a 6 leg flagship initiative strategy that combines informative gatherings, entrepreneurship development , arts and cultural awareness.
